Chrome Extension
PlanBridge for Chrome
Extract workouts from FITR with one click — no DevTools, no copy-pasting scripts
1
Download & unzip
Download the extension zip and unzip it anywhere on your computer.
2
Open Chrome Extensions
Go to chrome://extensions and enable Developer mode (top-right toggle).
3
Load the extension
Click Load unpacked and select the chrome-extension folder you unzipped.
4
Use it
Navigate to your FITR calendar, click the PlanBridge icon in Chrome, and hit Extract this month.
↓ Download Extension
The extension is not on the Chrome Web Store — it installs as an unpacked extension in Developer mode. Your data never leaves your machine: the extension only reads the FITR page and downloads a local file.
